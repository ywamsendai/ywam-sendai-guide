## 1. The Daily & Weekly Rhythm
Student life balances inward spiritual growth with outward ministry focus.
* **Intercession:** Minimum of 3 hours per week of corporate prayer for the nations.
* **Worship:** Regular corporate times focusing on God's nature and character.
* **One-on-Ones:** Regularly scheduled meetings with staff for personal mentoring.
* **Work Duties:** Practical service to maintain the community and hub.

---

## 2. Field Assignment (Outreach)
Following the lecture phase, students move into an 8-12 week cross-cultural field assignment.
* **Ministry Focus:** Minimum of 30 hours per week of outward-focused ministry serving those outside the team.
* **Team Time:** 15 hours per week of structured team building, debriefing, and prayer.
* **Primary Activity:** Evangelism, emphasizing meaningful connections to help people follow Jesus.

---

## 3. Evaluation & Completion
The DTS is a **[Pass/Fail](./standards)** course. To successfully complete the school, students are evaluated based on:
1.  **Participation:** Active engagement in all program activities and community life.
2.  **Character:** Adherence to the local YWAM code of conduct and foundational values.
3.  **Growth:** Evidence of deepened spiritual growth and relational health.
4.  **Requirements:** Completion of all assignments, including journal reflections and reading tasks.
