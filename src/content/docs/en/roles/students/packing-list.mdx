Coming to Sendai is an invitation to simplicity. We ask that you pack in a way that allows you to be mobile, as our "Outreach Phase" often requires moving between different locations in Tohoku.

---

## 1. The Stewardship of Luggage
To ensure we can travel together effectively, please limit yourself to:
* **One Primary Piece:** A hiking backpack or a medium suitcase (Max 80L). 
* **One Daypack:** A small everyday backpack for lectures and local ministry.

---

## 2. Essential Items
Please ensure you have the following before you depart:
* **Passport:** Valid for at least 6 months past your departure date.
* **Health Insurance:** Proof of coverage that is valid in Japan.
* **A Physical Bible:** We value the "analog" experience in our rhythms; please bring a paper Bible.
* **Seasonal Clothing:** Sendai has four distinct seasons. Check the forecast for "Miyagi Prefecture" before you pack.

---

## 3. Recommended for the Journey
* **National Gifts:** Small snacks or tokens from your home country are a beautiful way to practice hospitality.
* **The Sojourner's Kit:** A travel mug, a foldable umbrella, and a sleeping bag (essential for Outreach).

<Aside type="caution" title="Medical Readiness">
Ensure your routine immunizations (MMR, Polio, etc.) are up to date. While not legally required for entry to Japan, they are a wise stewardship of your health in a communal living environment.
</Aside>
