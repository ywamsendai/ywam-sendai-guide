Enrolling in a YWAM Sendai school is a journey of discernment. We use a three-stage process to move from your initial interest to your arrival in Tohoku.

## 1. Student Application Journey

<Steps>
1. **Initial Application** Fill out the [Application Form](https://ywamsendai.org/en/apply) and select "Student." Once submitted, we will automatically contact your provided references.

2. **Discernment & Prayer** After we receive your references, our school leadership team will pray and discuss your suitability for the specific school cycle. We prioritize spiritual hunger and a heart for Japan.

3. **Acceptance & Deposit** If accepted, you will receive an official acceptance message. To secure your place, a **[¥100,000 deposit](../fees-and-finances)** is required. This can be paid via [Wise or Bank Transfer](../../../community/payments). This deposit is non-refundable but counts toward your total tuition.

4. **Onboarding & Discord** Once the deposit is received, we will register you and invite you to our Discord server. This is where all pre-arrival communication, housing assignments, and preparations happen.
</Steps>

---

## 2. Student Logistic & Legal Preparation

<Steps>
1. **Passport Check:** Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months after your school's departure date.

2. **Visa Application:** Follow the specific guidance provided by our staff for your nationality (eVisa, Waiver, or Long-term).

3. **Flight Booking:** Aim to arrive at a Japanese airport (usually Sendai, Haneda, or Narita) on the **designated Arrival Date**. Send your itinerary to the staff.

4. **Insurance:** Secure travel/health insurance that explicitly covers medical care in Japan.
</Steps>

---

## 3. Student Final Preparations

<Steps>
1. **Financial Stewardship:** Pay the remaining [tuition fees by the deadline](../fees-and-finances).

2. **Packing:** Follow the [Packing List](../packing-list) guidelines—stay mobile and simple.

3. **Immunizations:** Ensure your routine immunizations (MMR, Polio, etc.) are up to date. While not legally required for entry to Japan, they are a wise stewardship of your health in a communal living environment.

4. **Pre-Arrival Reading:** Complete the assigned reading for your specific school (DTS/DBS).

5. **Language:** Learning some [basic Japanese phrases](../../../community/language) and hiragana go a long way in Sendai!
</Steps>

<Aside type="tip" title="Arrival Protocol">
24 hours before you land, check Discord for the latest Arrival info—this includes instructions on where to meet in Sendai.
</Aside>
