Enrolling in a YWAM Sendai school is a journey of discernment. We use a three-stage process to move from your initial interest to your arrival in Tohoku.

## The Application Journey

<Steps>
1. **Initial Application** Fill out the unified [Application Form](https://ywamsendai.org/en/apply) and select "Student." Once submitted, we will automatically contact your provided references.

2. **Discernment & Prayer** After we receive your references, our school leadership team will pray and discuss your suitability for the specific school cycle. We prioritize spiritual hunger and a heart for Japan.

3. **Acceptance & Deposit** If accepted, you will receive an official acceptance letter. To secure your place, a **¥100,000 deposit** is required. This deposit is non-refundable but counts toward your total tuition.

4. **Onboarding & Discord** Once the deposit is received, we will register you and invite you to our Discord server. This is where all pre-arrival communication, housing assignments, and preparations happen.
</Steps>

## Financial Reminders
* Full tuition must be paid prior to the first day of the Lecture Phase unless a prior payment plan is approved.
* Check the [Student Finances](./fees-and-finances) page for a breakdown of outreach costs.

## Preparation
We recommend starting your Japanese language study as soon as you are accepted. Even basic greetings go a long way in Sendai!
