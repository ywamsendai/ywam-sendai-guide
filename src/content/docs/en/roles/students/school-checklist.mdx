Once you have been accepted into a school, use this checklist to steward your preparation.

---

## 1. Immediate Actions (Post-Acceptance)
<Steps>
1. **Confirm Your Spot:** Reply to your acceptance message within 7 days to secure your place in the school.
2. **Deposit:** Pay your non-refundable deposit via Wise or Bank Transfer to activate your student file.
</Steps>

---

## 2. Logistics & Legal
<Steps>
1. **Passport Check:** Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months after your school's departure date.
2. **Visa Application:** Follow the specific guidance provided by our staff for your nationality (eVisa, Waiver, or Long-term).
3. **Flight Booking:** Aim to arrive at **Sendai International Airport (SDJ)** (or other airport) on the designated Arrival Date. Send your itinerary to the staff.
4. **Insurance:** Secure travel/health insurance that explicitly covers medical care in Japan.
</Steps>

---

## 3. Final Preparations
<Steps>
1. **Financial Stewardship:** Pay the remaining [tuition/housing fees by the deadline](../../../roles/students/fees-and-finances).
2. **Packing:** Follow the [Packing List](../../../roles/students/packing-list) guidelines—stay mobile and simple.
3. **Pre-Arrival Reading:** Complete the assigned reading for your specific school (DTS/DBS).
</Steps>

<Aside type="tip" title="Arrival Protocol">
24 hours before you land, check Discord for the latest Arrival info—this includes instructions on where to meet in Sendai.
</Aside>
