## 1. School Cost Breakdown
Whether you are attending a DTS or a Secondary School (like DBS), the base costs are divided into Tuition and Living Expenses.

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Tuition Fee" icon="pencil">
    **¥300,000** (Tax Included)
    Covers lectures, administration, and refreshments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Accommodation" icon="home">
    **¥120,000** Covers bedding, utilities, and high-speed Wi-Fi.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Living Budget" icon="setting">
    **Estimate: ¥150,000**
    Covers food (shared & individual), local train fares, and personal expenses.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Aside type="tip" title="The Combo Discount">
If you apply for both a DTS and DBS in the same year, you will receive a **¥50,000 discount** on the DBS tuition fee (pending successful DTS completion).
</Aside>

## 2. Outreach Phase (DTS Only)
The Outreach cost varies by location but generally includes transport, main meals, and housing.
* **Estimated Budget:** **¥400,000**
* **Note:** This does not include personal shopping or vaccination costs.

---

## 3. Payment Schedule & Methods
To ensure the school runs smoothly, we follow a strict payment timeline.

| Payment Item | Amount | Due Date | Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deposit** | ¥100,000 | Upon Acceptance | Wise / Bank |
| **Remaining Tuition** | ¥200,000 | 10 Days Before Arrival | Wise / Bank |
| **Accommodation** | ¥120,000 | **On Arrival** | **CASH ONLY** |
| **Outreach (Part 1)** | ¥150,000 | 1 Month Before Outreach | **CASH ONLY** |

<Aside type="danger" title="Important: Cash Payments">
In accordance with local operations, **Accommodation and Outreach fees must be paid in Japanese Yen (Cash) on-site.** Please ensure you have arranged for cash withdrawals through your home bank before arriving.
</Aside>

---

## 4. Refund Policy
* **Tuition:** Generally non-refundable once paid.
* **Accommodation/Food:** Pro-rated refund by the week.
* **Outreach:** Full amount minus airline cancellation fees and non-recoverable expenses already paid by the base.
