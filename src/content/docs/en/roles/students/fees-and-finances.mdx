## 1. Lecture Phase Costs (DTS & DBS)
The Lecture Phase costs are identical for both the Discipleship Training School (DTS) and the Discipleship Bible School (DBS). 

**Total Lecture Phase: ¥570,000**

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Tuition Fee" icon="pencil">
    **¥300,000** (Tax Included)
    Covers guest speakers, school administration, and refreshments.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Accommodation" icon="home">
    **¥120,000** Covers housing at the YWAM Sendai base, utilities, and Wi-Fi.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Living Budget" icon="setting">
    **Estimate: ¥150,000**
    Covers food (shared and individual meals) and local transportation.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Aside type="tip" title="The Combo Discount">
If you apply for both a DTS and DBS in the same year, you will receive a **¥50,000 discount** on the DBS tuition fee.
</Aside>

## 2. Outreach Phase Costs (Mandatory for DTS Only)
**Important:** The Outreach phase is a required part of the DTS, but it does **not** exist for the DBS (which is a lecture-only school).

* **Estimated DTS Outreach Budget:** **¥400,000**
* **Inclusions:** Typically covers international/domestic airfare, ministry housing, and basic meals.
* **Exclusions:** Personal shopping, souvenirs, or required vaccinations.

## 4. Payment Schedule & International Methods
All students must follow this timeline to ensure base operations. For technical help with transfers, see our [International Payment Guide](/community/finance).

| Item | Amount | Due Date | Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Deposit** | ¥100,000 | Upon Acceptance | Wise / Bank Transfer |
| **Balance** | ¥200,000 | 10 Days Before Arrival | Wise / Bank Transfer |
| **Housing** | ¥120,000 | **On Day 1** | **CASH ONLY (JPY)** |
| **Outreach Deposit** | ¥150,000 | 1 Month Before Outreach | **CASH ONLY (JPY)** |
| **Outreach Balance** | ¥250,000 | 2 Weeks Before Outreach | **CASH ONLY (JPY)** |

<Aside type="danger" title="Japan is a Cash Society">
Accommodation and Outreach fees **must be paid in Japanese Yen (Cash)** on-site. Please review our [Finance Guide](/community/finance) for advice on withdrawing large sums of cash in Sendai.
</Aside>

---

## 5. Refund Policy
* **Tuition:** Non-refundable once the school begins.
* **Housing:** Pro-rated weekly if a student leaves the school early.
* **Outreach:** Full refund minus non-recoverable costs (like booked flights).
