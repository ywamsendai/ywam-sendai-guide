We love hosting teams and individuals who want to serve the Tohoku region!

## 1. Outreach Teams
If you are a YWAM leader looking to bring a team for an outreach:
1. **The Form:** The Team Leader should fill out the "Team" application on our [main form](https://ywamsendai.org/en/apply).
2. **Interview:** We will schedule a call with the Team Leader to discuss dates, ministry focus, and housing.
3. **Approval:** Once approved, we will provide a "Team Packet" with logistics for your specific dates.

## 2. Individual Visitors
For individuals wanting to visit for 1–2 weeks:
1. **The Form:** Select "Individual Visitor" on the main application form.
2. **Acceptance:** Upon review, we will confirm if we have space in the Hub for your requested dates.

## 3. Visas for Short-Term
Most visitors enter Japan on a **90-day Visa Waiver** or an **eVisa**. We do not provide sponsorship for short-term visits; please ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months.
