As YWAM Japan is a registered Religious Corporation, we can assist foreign staff in receiving a Religious Activities Visa. This process begins with the **Certificate of Eligibility (CoE)**.

---

## 1. The Timeline
The process usually takes **6 to 12 weeks** for immigration to process, plus shipping and embassy time. Do not book your flights until your visa is physically in your passport.

---

## 2. The Process
<Steps>
1. **Financial Milestone:** Inform leadership once you have reached your **Minimum Support Level**.
2. **Form Submission:** We will send you a Visa Information form to complete.
3. **Document Delivery:** Send all required documents (listed below) via email and physical mail.
4. **Immigration Filing:** We apply for the CoE at the Japanese Immigration Bureau.
5. **Embassy Visit:** Once we mail the CoE to you, take it to your local Japanese Embassy/Consulate with your passport and the visa application form.
6. **Arrival:** You must enter Japan within **3 months** of the CoE's issue date.
</Steps>

---

## 3. Required Documents
Please prepare the following. All non-English/Japanese documents must include a translation.

* **Passport Copy:** Clear scan of the photo page.
* **Portrait Photo:** One 4cm x 3cm photo (plain background, taken within 3 months).
* **Church Letter of Dispatch:** Original, signed on church stationery.
* **Church Letter of Release:** Original, signed on church stationery.

<Aside type="note" title="A Note for Your Church">
The Letter of Release states the church provides financial support. While you likely have many supporters, Japanese Immigration requires one entity to "vouch" for you to ensure you are not destitute. This is a standard requirement and is not a legally binding contract for the church to pay all your expenses.
</Aside>
