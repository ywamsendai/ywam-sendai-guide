Being on staff at YWAM Sendai is a calling to a **Rule of Life**. We recognize that our external ministry to Tohoku is only as fruitful as our internal **Union with God (Theosis)**. This covenant defines the heart and boundaries of our shared life.

---

## I. Spiritual Commitment: The Interior Life
We believe that "doing" must flow from "being." As a member of this staff, I commit to:
* **The Inward Journey:** Prioritizing a daily liturgy of prayer, Scripture, and worship.
* **The YWAM DNA:** Embodying the [Foundational Values](../../../foundations/ywam) and maintaining the [Covenants](../../../foundations/legacy-covenants) of our global movement.
* **Spiritual Unity:** Protecting the "Shalom" of the house by resolving conflict according to the "Matthew 18" principle—speaking directly and in love.

## II. The Sacredness of the Person
We uphold a **Holistic Sanctity**, honoring God with our whole selves:

We recognize that longevity in the mission field requires physical stewardship. I commit to pursuing excellence in rest, movement, and nutrition as acts of worship.
</Aside>

## III. The Heart for Japan: Cultural Humility
To love Sendai is to speak its heart language. I commit to the journey of **Incarnational Ministry**:
* **Language Pursuit:** I commit to the [Language Rhythms](../../../community/language-learning) of the house, pursuing **N2 Fluency** as a tool for deeper relationship with the Japanese people.
* **Cultural Immersion:** I commit to learning from the Japanese church and social structures, approaching the culture with the heart of a servant.

## IV. Communal Stewardship
Our mission is built on **Presence**. I commit to:
* **Showing Up:** Being fully present for the [Core Rhythms](../../../community/rhythms-of-life) of the community.
* **Bilingual Unity:** Favoring Japanese as our common language in shared spaces to foster an environment of growth.
* **Digital Diligence:** Utilizing our communication tools (Discord) to stay aligned and connected with the team.

## V. Financial & Legal Integrity
We are a "supported" movement, and we operate with transparency before God and the State:
* **Base Contribution:** I commit to the monthly **¥6,000 contribution** for the stewardship of our shared mission.
* **Legal Honor:** I commit to fulfilling my obligations to the Japanese State, including **National Health Insurance** and **National Pension**, protecting the reputation of the community.

---

### The Commitment
*By continuing in this role, I am not signing a contract, but entering a covenant. I choose to walk this path with my brothers and sisters in Sendai for the glory of God and the renewal of Tohoku.*
