**Note:** This is the legal process for your Japanese Visa. You must first be accepted as staff through our [Staff Onboarding](../onboarding) process before starting these steps.

## 1. Timeline & Flights
The **Certificate of Eligibility (CoE)** process usually takes **6 to 12 weeks**. 
* **Crucial Rule:** Do not book non-refundable flights until the visa is approved. Issuance is not guaranteed.

## 2. The Legal Step-by-Step
<Steps>
1. **Financial Milestone:** Reach your **[Support Goal](../stewardship-finance)** and notify us, and include an expected arrival period you are aiming for.
2. **CoE Application Information:** Complete the "[CoE Information Form](https://ywamsendai.org/en/coe)".
3. **Gather Documents:** Gather and send the required documents (listed below) to us.
4. **CoE Application and Review:** We file the paperwork in Japan and await Immigration processing. They may request additional documents during this time.
5. **CoE Issuance and Visa Application:** Once issued, we send a PDF copy of the CoE to you. You apply for your visa at your local Japanese embassy or consulate.
6. **Arrival:** You must enter Japan within **3 months** of the CoE issue date.
</Steps>

## 3. Required Documents for CoE
Please send high-resolution colour scans of the following, in PDF format unless otherwise stated:
* **Passport Copy:** The passport identification page (if the name differs from other documents, **please tell us immediately**).
* **Portrait Photo:** 4cm x 3cm (plain background, clear, front-facing, no hat, taken within 6 months). High resolution jpeg file.
* **[Dispatch Letter from sending organization](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lxulVT3zbsav8C8czrh-0BKM7FlXP-ekxYtn7zQK67g/edit?usp=sharing):** Signed on official letterhead.
* **Ministry Certification:** Any YWAM UofN (e.g. DTS), other ministry training, or ordination/commissioning certificates you have.
* **Overview of Sending Organization:** Document(s) outlining the denomination/affiliation, history, leadership, organizational structure, facilities, approx. membership, and ministry activities of the sending organization.
* **Dependent Documentation (if applicable):** If you have children or anyone else who will need a dependent visa, we may also need birth certificates, passport copies, and photos.
