**Note:** This is the legal process for your Japanese Visa. You must first be accepted as staff through our [Staff Onboarding](./onboarding) process before starting these steps.

## 1. Timeline & Flights
The **Certificate of Eligibility (CoE)** process usually takes **6 to 12 weeks**. 
* **Crucial Rule:** Do not book your flights until the visa is physically in your passport.

## 2. The Legal Step-by-Step
<Steps>
1. **Financial Milestone:** Reach your **Minimum Support Level** and notify leadership.
2. **Visa Data Form:** Complete the "Visa Information Form" we send you.
3. **Document Courier:** Mail your physical documents (listed below) to the Sendai Hub.
4. **CoE Issuance:** We file the paperwork in Japan. Once issued, we mail the original CoE to you.
5. **Embassy Visit:** Take the original CoE to your local Japanese Embassy to receive the visa stamp.
6. **Arrival:** You must enter Japan within **3 months** of the CoE issue date.
</Steps>

## 3. Required Documents for CoE
* **Passport Copy:** High-resolution scan of the photo page.
* **Portrait Photo:** 4cm x 3cm (plain background, taken within 3 months).
* **[Church Letter of Dispatch](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lxulVT3zbsav8C8czrh-0BKM7FlXP-ekxYtn7zQK67g/edit?usp=sharing):** Original signed document on church letterhead.
* **[Church Letter of Release](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lxulVT3zbsav8C8czrh-0BKM7FlXP-ekxYtn7zQK67g/edit?usp=sharing):** Original signed document on church letterhead.

<Aside type="note" title="Financial Vouching">
The "Letter of Release" is a standard Japanese requirement for one entity to "vouch" for your support. It is not a legally binding contract for the church to pay all your expenses, but proof you won't be destitute.
</Aside>
