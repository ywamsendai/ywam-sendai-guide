Joining YWAM Sendai as staff is a commitment to a pioneering, "modern monastic" community. We are looking for flexible, supernatural-lifestyle-oriented leaders.

<Aside type="caution" title="Prerequisite">
You must have successfully completed a YWAM Discipleship Training School (DTS) at any registered UofN location to apply for staff.
</Aside>

## The Staff Journey

<Steps>
1. **Connect & Explore** Before applying, join our Discord, introduce yourself, and read through our [Staff Covenant and Expectations](../covenant). We want you to know our DNA before you commit.

2. **The Application & Interview** Submit the unified [form](https://ywamsendai.org/en/apply) selecting "Staff." We will then schedule a video interview to discern your suitability for a pioneering environment.

3. **References & Decision** We will contact your references for a fuller perspective on your character and gifting. Once we have a clear "Yes" from our leadership team, you will be notified.

4. **Fundraising & Visa** Once accepted, you must begin your partnership development. You can officially join the team in Sendai once you reach your **Minimum Support Level**. If you require a visa, we will begin the legal paperwork at this stage.
</Steps>

## Preparation
* **Language:** Staff are expected to be active learners of the Japanese language.
* **Finances:** All staff are self-supported. See [Staff Stewardship](../stewardship-finance) for more details.
