You can begin your visa renewal **3 months** before expiration. 

## 1. Renewal Steps
<Steps>
1. **Internal Request:** Message us with your current residence card details.
2. **National Office Request:** We will request the necessary documents from the YWAM Japan National Office.
3. **Immigration Visit:** Take your documents to the Sendai Immigration Office.
4. **Card Collection:** Pay the **¥6,000 revenue stamp** (shunyu inshi) and collect your new card.
</Steps>

## 2. Required Documents (Renewal)
* **Passport Copy:** The passport identification page.
* **Residence Card Copy:** Front and back.
* **Portrait Photo:** 4cm x 3cm (plain background, clear, front-facing, no hat, taken within 6 months).
* **Tax Certificates:** *Nouzei Shoumeisho* and *Kazei Shoumeisho* from your local Sendai City Office.
* **Application Form:** You complete pages 1 & 2; YWAM Sendai completes page 3.
* **[Continuation of Dispatch Letter](https://docs.google.com/document/d/15FPBrqX3iIlqiNkz2ZI-qp6mgSea32VgcmpDND52roM/edit?usp=sharing):** New signed letter from your sending organization.
