You can begin the renewal process **3 months** before your current visa expires. We recommend gathering documents at the 4-month mark.

---

## 1. The Process
<Steps>
1. **Internal Notification:** Message the Base Leader with your residence card number and your sending church’s current name/address.
2. **Document Gathering:** Collect all items in the list below.
3. **Submission:** Submit your application at the Sendai Immigration Office (or online via the immigration portal).
4. **Collection:** After 2–4 weeks, you will receive a postcard. Take this to the office with a **¥6,000 revenue stamp** to collect your new card.
</Steps>

---

## 2. Required Documents
* **Application Form:** Pages 1 & 2 (completed by you) and Page 3 (completed by YWAM).
* **Invitation/Warranty:** Issued by YWAM Japan National Office.
* **Tax Certificates:** *Nouzei Shoumeisho* and *Kazei Shoumeisho* from your local City Office.
* **Church Letters:** Original, signed Letter of Dispatch and Letter of Release.
* **Portrait Photo:** 4cm x 3cm, name written on the back.
* **Current ID:** Original Passport and Residence Card.

<Aside type="caution" title="Revenue Stamp">
The ¥6,000 fee is paid via a "Revenue Stamp" (*shunyu inshi*), which can be purchased at any Post Office or inside the convenience store at the Immigration Bureau.
</Aside>
