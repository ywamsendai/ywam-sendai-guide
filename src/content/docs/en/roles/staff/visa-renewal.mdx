You can begin your visa renewal **3 months** before expiration. 

## 1. Renewal Steps
<Steps>
1. **Internal Request:** Message the Base Leader with your current residence card details.
2. **National Office Request:** We will request the necessary warranty documents from the YWAM Japan National Office.
3. **Immigration Visit:** Take your documents to the Sendai Immigration Office.
4. **Card Collection:** Pay the **¥6,000 revenue stamp** (shunyu inshi) and collect your new card.
</Steps>

## 2. Required Documents (Renewal)
* **Tax Certificates:** *Nouzei Shoumeisho* and *Kazei Shoumeisho* from your local Sendai City Office.
* **Application Form:** You complete pages 1 & 2; YWAM Sendai completes page 3.
* **Updated [Church Letters](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lxulVT3zbsav8C8czrh-0BKM7FlXP-ekxYtn7zQK67g/edit?usp=sharing):** New signed originals from your sending church.
