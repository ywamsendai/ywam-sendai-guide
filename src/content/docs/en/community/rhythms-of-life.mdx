# Rhythms of Life

In Sendai, we steward time through the lens of **Incarnational Presence** and **Spiritual Devotion**. We reject the "culture of busyness" in favor of a rhythm that sustains long-term fruitfulness in Tohoku.

---

## 1. The Weekend Rhythm: Sabbath & Sunday
We distinguish between **Personal Rest** and **Communal Worship**.

* **Saturday (The Sabbath):** A day of personal "ceasing." We refrain from work and digital pings to delight in God and His creation.
* **Sunday (Worship & Service):** We integrate with the local Japanese Church, fellowshipping with believers and serving our neighbors.

## 3. Time Stewardship for All
Regardless of your role, we encourage a rhythm that protects your health:
* **Digital Boundaries:** We observe a base-wide [Digital Curfew](../../operations/digital) at 21:00.
* **National Holidays:** We honour all Japanese National Holidays as days of rest and cultural alignment.

<Aside type="tip" title="Quality over Quantity">
We prioritize the **quality of our presence** over the number of hours spent working. We believe a rested, whole person is the best witness to the Gospel in Sendai.
</Aside>
