We reject the "culture of busyness." Our time stewardship is focused on **Incarnational Presence**—being fully present with God and the people of Tohoku.

## 1. Weekly Rhythms
These are the steady beats of our community life.

* **Monday @10:00:** Worship & Intercession (Community centering).
* **Wednesday Evenings:** Entertainment District Ministry.
* **Friday Mornings:** Homeless Ministry.
* **Saturdays:** Personal Sabbath (Ceasing from work/digital).
* **Sundays:** Local Japanese Church involvement.

## 2. Monthly & Biweekly Rhythms
* **The Common Table (Monthly):** Community celebration meal.
* **Student Ministry (Monthly):** University outreach and friendship building.
* **Japanese Time (Biweekly):** Dedicated language practice and cultural learning.

## 3. Seasonal Rest
* **National Holidays:** We honor all Japanese National Holidays as days of rest.
* **Selah Seasons:** Between school terms, we prioritize a slower pace for base maintenance and staff recovery.

<Aside type="tip" title="Quality over Quantity">
We prioritize the **quality of our presence** over the number of hours spent. A rested person is a more effective missionary.
</Aside>
