## 3. How to Withdraw Cash in Japan
As noted in the School Finance guide, accommodation and outreach fees must be paid in **Cash (JPY)**.

**Recommended Methods for International Travelers:**
1. **7-Eleven ATMs (7-Bank):** These are the most reliable for foreign debit/credit cards. They are open 24/7 and have a full English interface.
2. **Japan Post ATMs:** Located in most post offices, these also reliably accept international cards (Visa, Plus, Mastercard, Maestro).

<Aside type="tip" title="Daily Limits">
Most Japanese ATMs have a 100,000 JPY daily withdrawal limit. If you need to pay 120,000 JPY for housing, you may need to withdraw over two separate days. **Notify your home bank** before traveling to ensure your card is active for Japan.
</Aside>
