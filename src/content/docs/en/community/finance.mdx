## 2. Domestic Payments (Within Japan)
If you are already in Japan or have a local bank account, you may use our Japan Post (Yucho) details.

**Japan Post Bank (ゆうちょ銀行):**
* **Symbol (Kigo):** 10140
* **Number (Bango):** 65305041
* **Name:** セカイセイネンセンキョウカイ

<Steps>
1. **At the ATM:** Select "English" > "Remittance."
2. **Enter Details:** Input the Kigo (10140) and Bango (65305041).
3. **Confirm:** Verify the name appears as "セカイセイネンセンキョウカイ."
4. **Save:** We recommend saving this account for future monthly fees or donations.
</Steps>

## 4. Notification of Transfer
To help our finance team track your payment, please send a quick email to **finance@ywamsendai.org** with:
* The Date
* The Amount
* The Purpose (e.g., "DTS Tuition," "Staff Fees," or "Property Fund Donation")
