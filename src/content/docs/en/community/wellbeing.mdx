In Sendai, we believe that high-level ministry requires high-level stewardship of our bodies and minds. Our "Modern Monastic" identity is rooted in the belief that a rested, whole person is the best witness to the Gospel.

## 1. Digital Hygiene & Sabbath
To protect our interior silence and communal presence, we observe specific digital boundaries.

### The Saturday Digital Sabbath
We observe a total **Digital Fast** from **Friday Sunset to Sunday Morning**.
* **The Goal:** Total withdrawal from ministry-related digital noise.
* **Protocol:** No Discord pings, ministry emails, or work-related messages during this window.

### Daily Digital Curfew
* **21:00 Curfew:** Refrain from sending non-emergency Discord messages after 21:00.
* **Sleep Hygiene:** We encourage disconnecting from screens at least 60 minutes before sleep to protect circadian rhythms and ensure cognitive recovery.

## 2. Longevity & Nutrition
Aligned with our longevity values, the "Common Table" in Sendai prioritizes:
* **Whole Foods:** We use local, seasonal Miyagi produce and avoid ultra-processed additives.
* **Traditional Ingredients:** We prioritize real miso, shoyu, and mirin.
* **Dietary Stewardship:** If you have allergies, please disclose them during onboarding. We strive for a clean, whole-food menu for the community.

## 3. Physical Safety & Healthcare
* **Emergencies:** Dial **119** and say **"Kyūkyū"** (Ambulance). Notify base leadership immediately.
* **Abuse Prevention:** We maintain a zero-tolerance policy. If you witness or experience misconduct, contact leadership via our confidential channels.
* **Safeguarding:** We follow the "Rule of Two"—avoid being alone with students or children in private rooms.
* **Local Clinics:** There are several excellent clinics within walking distance.

## 4. Earthquake Readiness
As we reside in a seismic zone, every member must adhere to these safety standards:
* **Go-Bags:** Every resident must maintain a 3-day emergency bag (water, food, medicine).
* **Anchoring:** All heavy furniture must be secured to walls.
* **Evacuation:** Know the designated Aoba-ku evacuation area for our block.
