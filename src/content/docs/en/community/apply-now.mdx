Whether you are applying for a school, a staff position, a team outreach, or an individual visit, our process begins at the same place.

### The Unified Application Form
We use a single, intelligent application form for all roles. Based on your selection, the questions will tailor themselves to your specific journey.

[Apply to YWAM Sendai Here](https://ywamsendai.org/en/apply)

## Eligibility at a Glance
* **Minimum Age:** 18 years old (You may apply at 17 if you turn 18 during your time with us).
* **Language:** Proficiency in either English or Japanese is required.
* **Staff Requirement:** You must have successfully completed a YWAM DTS to apply for staff.

* **Students:** See the [DTS/DBS Application Path](../../roles/students/application-process).
