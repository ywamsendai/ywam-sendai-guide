## 1. National Office Coordination
YWAM Sendai operates under the YWAM Japan Religious Corporation (Shūkyō Hōjin).
* **Timeline:** Allow **14 days** when requesting a "Letter of Warranty" from the National Office.
* **Verification:** Verify the staff member has reached **Minimum Support Level** before requesting sponsorship.

## 2. Document Standards & Security
* **Role Alignment:** Ensure the applicant's "Activity Description" aligns with Religious Activities (evangelism, education, etc.).
* **Data Privacy:** Store all passport scans and tax records in the encrypted "Personnel" folder. Physical copies must be in the locked filing cabinet.

## 3. Template Management
A template for the letters of Dispatch and Release is kept on [Google Drive](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1lxulVT3zbsav8C8czrh-0BKM7FlXP-ekxYtn7zQK67g/edit?usp=sharing).
