The Sendai Hub is a tool for ministry. Stewarding this facility well allows us to host others safely and effectively.

---

## 1. Earthquake Readiness (Tohoku Standard)
Living in Sendai means being prepared for seismic activity.
* **Emergency Bags:** Every resident (staff and student) must have a "Go-Bag" containing 3 days of water, food, and basic medical supplies.
* **Furniture Anchoring:** All heavy shelves and appliances in the Hub must be secured to the walls to prevent injury during a quake.
* **The Evacuation Plan:** Familiarize yourself with the designated evacuation area for our specific block in Aoba-ku.

---

## 2. Maintenance & Stewardship
* **Work Duties:** Daily maintenance is handled via the [Student Standards](../../roles/students/standards) work duties.
* **Reporting Damage:** If something breaks (plumbing, electrical, or structural), report it immediately on the **#facilities** Discord channel.
* **Energy Stewardship:** Electricity in Japan is expensive. Please ensure all lights and AC units are turned off when rooms are not in use.

---

## 3. Safeguarding Policy
We are committed to being a safe community for everyone, especially vulnerable individuals.
* **The Rule of Two:** Avoid being alone with a student or child in a private room. Meetings should happen in public or semi-public spaces.
* **Reporting:** Any suspicion of abuse or misconduct must be reported directly to the Base Leadership immediately.

<Aside type="danger" title="Fire Safety">
Open flames (including candles) are strictly prohibited in bedrooms due to the high density of Japanese wooden structures.
</Aside>
