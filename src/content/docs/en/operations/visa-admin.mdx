## 1. National Office Coordination
Since we operate under the YWAM Japan Shūkyō Hōjin, all new visa (CoE) applications and certain renewals require coordination with the National Office.

* **Primary Contact:** [Insert National Office Admin Name/Email]
* **Requesting Documents:** When requesting the "Letter of Warranty" or "Corporation Registration" from the National Office, allow at least **14 days** for processing.

---

## 2. Sponsorship Integrity
To protect our legal status, we must ensure:
* **Role Accuracy:** Staff roles must align with the "Religious Activities" category (evangelism, religious education, etc.).
* **Financial Thresholds:** We must verify that a staff member has reached their **Minimum Support Level** before the base officially signs the warranty.
