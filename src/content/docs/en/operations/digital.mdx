Our digital tools are servants, not masters. We use them to foster bilingual unity while protecting the "monastic science" of deep rest and focus.

---

## 1. Discord: Our Virtual Hub
To prevent notification fatigue, we follow these protocols:

* **Asynchronous Expectations:** Reply to non-emergency pings within **24 hours** (Monday–Friday). 
* **Status Indicators:** Use your status to signal your current rhythm (e.g., 🟢 Available, ⛔ Deep Work, 🌙 Sabbath).
* **Thread Usage:** Use Discord "Threads" for specific technical or school-related discussions to keep main channels clear and scannable.

---

## 2. GitHub: Our Source of Truth
* **Documentation Rule:** A policy isn't "official" until the Pull Request is merged into this Guide.
* **Contribution:** All staff are encouraged to suggest edits via GitHub to ensure the Guide reflects our shared experience in Sendai.
