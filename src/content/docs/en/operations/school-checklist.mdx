---

## 1. Phase: Preparation & Admissions (6–3 Months Before)
*Focus: Recruiting and Visa Strategy.*

* [ ] **The 90-Day Strategy:** Ensure the school schedule fits within the **90-day Visa Waiver** window for eligible nations.
* [ ] **Acceptance Packets:** Send official acceptance letters and the "Arrival Guide." 
* [ ] **Travel Guidance:** Remind students to book return flights that fall within the 90-day window to comply with Japanese Immigration.
* [ ] **UofN Form A:** Register the school with the University of the Nations.

---

## 2. Phase: Pre-Launch (1 Month Before)
*Focus: Final logistics.*

* [ ] **Lecture Phase Schedule:** Finalize the 12-week speaker list.
* [ ] **Finance Setup:** Create a specific school sub-folder in the accounting drive.
* [ ] **Outreach Scouting (DTS Only):** Finalize the 4-week Overseas and 4-week Tohoku locations.

---

## 3. Phase: The Lecture Phase (Weeks 1–12)
*Focus: Maintaining the [Rhythms of Life](../../community/rhythms-of-life).*

* [ ] **Mid-Term Reset (Week 7):** Implement the restorative "Selah" week for students and staff.
* [ ] **DTS Outreach Logistics:** Finalize flights for the overseas phase and transport for the Tohoku phase.
* [ ] **DBS Final Paperwork:** For DBS, begin prep for Graduation in Week 12.

---

## 4. Phase: The Outreach & Debrief (DTS Only)
*Focus: Off-site management and re-entry.*

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="Weeks 13–16: Overseas">
    * [ ] **Travel Insurance:** Verify all students/staff have active international coverage.
    * [ ] **Crisis Prep:** Ensure outreach leaders have the "Emergency Contact Tree."
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Weeks 17–20: Tohoku">
    * [ ] **Regional Logistics:** Finalize housing and local ministry partnerships within the Tohoku region.
    * [ ] **Financial Tracking:** Collect all receipts from regional travel.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Week 21: Debriefing">
    * [ ] **Re-entry:** Final week in Sendai for processing, testimony, and graduation.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

---

## 5. Phase: Wrap-Up & Reporting (Post-School)
*Focus: Legal closure and record-keeping.*

* [ ] **Final Accounting:** Reconcile all school fees. Note: Outreach fees are often handled separately per the [Financial Procedures](../finance).
* [ ] **UofN Form B:** Submit final grades to the UofN.
* [ ] **Student Files:** Move all records to secure digital archiving.
