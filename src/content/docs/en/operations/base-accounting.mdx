These protocols ensure our base remains in full alignment with the **YWAM Japan Shukyo Houjin** national standards. All financial activities must be recorded transparently to maintain our collective integrity.

---

## 1. The Fiscal Cycle
Accuracy in our annual reporting is a legal requirement for the national religious corporation.

* **Fiscal Year:** April 1st to March 31st.
* **Synchronization Deadline:** Local backups must be sent to the National Accounting Office (Goto-sensei) by **April 15th** annually.

---

## 2. Software: Kaikeiou (会計王)
We utilize the national standard software for all base-level entries.

<Steps>
1.  **System Requirements:** Windows 11 with the **Japanese Language Pack** installed (mandatory for database rendering).
2.  **Backup Protocol:** Weekly backups should be stored on the secure base drive.
3.  **Data Integrity:** Ensure the local "OpLoc" code is correctly set to Sendai before entering transactions.
</Steps>

## 3. Chart of Accounts (Income)
All incoming funds must be categorized according to the **YWAM Japan Accounting Handbook**.

| Category | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Donation Income** | All donations from individuals or organisations. **Includes Staff Monthly Contributions (¥6,000) and guest accommodation.** |
| **Interest Income** | Bank account interest. |
| **Misc Income** | All other income. Includes school fees. |

---

## 4. Chart of Accounts (Expenses)
Standardized categories for all base expenditures.

| Category | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Communication** | Ministry-related communication costs or fees (e.g. internet, online service subscriptions, postage, telephone, etc; but not TV-related costs). |
| **Consumable Supplies** | Expendable items which cost under ¥100,000 per item. (e.g. electronics, household items, other small items, etc.) |
| **Donations** | Donations to an organisation/association/society/group. The recipient of the donation must be specified clearly. (But not to an individual) |
| **Equipment Purchase** | Purchase of items that will last at least one year and that costs ¥100,000 or more. |
| **Hospitality** | Gifts for people outside YWAM including seasonal gifts, flowers for funerals, congratulatory gifts, etc. (But not food and drink) |
| **Insurance** | Insurance related to ministry. |
| **Meetings** | Meeting-related expenses, including some meals and refreshments. (¥5,000 or less per person.) Write the names of the people in the meeting in the explanation space. |
| **Membership/Conference Fees** | Ministry-related membership fees (for associations, clubs, etc.), seminar registration fees, etc. |
| **Ministry Prep** | General expenses related to preparation for ministry activities. Includes advertising costs and expenses other than transportation costs to have a speaker for DTS, etc. (Food expenses for a DTS or ministry with non-staff people are acceptable, but other food costs for staff are not). |
| **Misc Expense** | Small expenses not fitting into the other categories. Includes bank handling charges, tokibotohon (copy of our religious body registration paper) costs and inkanshomeisho (seal registration) costs. Also any money sent to another OpLoc/ministry in YWAM Japan, including National Office Fees. |
| **Office Supply** | Expendable office supplies such as paper, writing utensils, computer accessories, etc. |
| **Remuneration** | Fees paid to teachers, tax accountants and legal advisors, and honorariums given to guest speakers, translators, etc. |
| **Remuneration (Tax)** | Withholding tax on remuneration paid. |
| **Rental Payment** | Rental or lease payments (not including rental cars). Write the address of a property rented in YWAM’s name the first time the expense appears in the year. |
| **Repairs** | Expenses related to maintenance and repairs (includes light bulbs, disposal of large trash items, etc.) |
| **Travel/Transportation** | Expenses for ministry related travel or transportation (excluding expenses falling into the Vehicle category). |
| **Utilities** | Electricity, gas, kerosene, water, etc. (Not including Internet and phone, which are Communications expenses.) |
| **Vehicle** | General expenses for ministry related vehicle use. This includes gasoline, road tolls, parking, and rental cars. |

## 5. Specific Protocols
* **Internal Transfers:** Transfers to other YWAM Japan OpLocs must be clearly tagged as "Internal" to avoid being flagged as external income.
* **Receipt Management:** Every expense must have a physical or digital receipt. For purchases over **¥5,000**, please consult leadership before proceeding.
* **Withholding Tax:** Tax must be withheld from any honorariums paid (3.063% for Japan-resident YWAM staff, 10.21% for Japan-resident non-YWAM staff, and 20.42% for all resident overseas). This is a strict legal requirement of the Shukyo Houjin.
