# Onboarding Philosophy

We believe that the application process is the first act of hospitality. We reject the "Cold Form" in favor of the "Warm Dialogue." 

---

## 1. The Relational Funnel
We utilize a digital-to-human pipeline to steward our time and the applicant's heart.

<Steps>
1.  **The Digital Host:** An AI-driven chat (via Instagram or LINE) gathers baseline data and assesses cultural alignment through natural conversation.
2.  **The Discernment Summary:** The AI generates a "Heart Profile" and summarizes the applicant's journey for the staff team.
3.  **The Relational Web (References):** Instead of a form, we reach out to the applicant’s mentors via their preferred chat app to hear their perspective on the applicant's communal fit.
4.  **The Human Connection:** Direct video call with a staff member to confirm the call and build a personal relationship.
5.  **The Administrative Seal:** Formalizing legal/financial requirements (Visas, Fees) via a personalized dashboard.
</Steps>

---

## 2. Standards for Engagement
When a staff member takes over a chat from the AI, we observe these standards:
* **Response Time:** Aim for a "Human Ping" within 24 hours of the AI handoff.
* **Tone:** Maintain the [Sendai Voice](../branding)—rooted, clear, and hopeful. 
* **Transparency:** Be honest about the "Hard Soil" of Tohoku. We don't "sell" a school; we invite them into a vocation.

---

## 3. The Relational Web (References)
We view references not as a background check, but as a way to see the applicant through the eyes of their home community.
* **Frictionless Entry:** We prefer voice notes or short chat replies from mentors over long forms.
* **Communal Fit:** We specifically ask about the applicant's capacity for the "Monastic" (solitude/focus) and the "Missional" (hospitality/grit).

---

## 4. The Onboarding Dashboard
Once accepted, every member receives a digital "Key to the Hub." This is a curated list of their specific requirements:
* **Legal:** Visa status and insurance.
* **Community:** Reading list and [Rhythms of Life](../../community/rhythms-of-life).
* **Practical:** Packing lists and station arrival protocols.

---

## 5. Application Essentials
* **Age:** Minimum 18 years old (you may apply at 17 if you turn 18 by the end of the course).
* **Language:** Proficiency in either English or Japanese is required.
* **Visas:** Most short-term attendees enter on a 90-day Visa Waiver or an eVisa. We provide specific guidance once you are accepted.

<Aside type="tip" title="The Goal">
The goal of onboarding is to move someone from "Stranger" to "Family" before they even land at Sendai Station.
</Aside>
