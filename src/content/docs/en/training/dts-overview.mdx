The Discipleship Training School (YWAM DTS) is a five to six-month, full-time Christian training program designed to help you discover your passions and your part in God's purposes for the world.

---

## 1. Purpose of the DTS
The DTS at YWAM Sendai is built on five core pillars of purpose:
* **Gather & Challenge:** Releasing people to serve through evangelism, intercession, and acts of compassion.
* **Cultivate Growth:** Building a relational foundation with God and developing Christ-like character based on Biblical truth.
* **Sharpen Perspective:** Improving the ability to relate to and work with people from different cultures, personalities, and perspectives.
* **Equip for Service:** Strengthening a commitment to reach the unreached, care for the poor, and influence all areas of society.
* **Impart Vision:** Sharing the foundational values of YWAM and providing information regarding global opportunities for service.

---

## 2. The "Live-Learn" Philosophy
The DTS is more than a classroom; it is a live-learn residential environment set within a ministry context. It blends:
* Classroom learning and small group processing.
* Personal reflection and practical service.
* Community living and one-on-one mentoring.

---

## 3. Learning Goals
Students will aim to understand "God's Big Story" from Creation to redemption while developing the ability to listen to and obey God's voice in daily life.
