The DTS is divided into two primary phases: the **Lecture Phase** and the **Field Assignment (Outreach) Phase**.

---

## 1. Required Teaching Units
To provide a solid Biblical foundation, the curriculum covers these core content areas:
1.  **The Bible:** Understanding Scripture as God's primary standard for living.
2.  **Who is God:** Exploring the Trinitarian nature, character, and ways of God.
3.  **In the Beginning:** Recognizing human identity as God's image-bearers.
4.  **What Went Wrong:** Understanding the impact of sin and the rebellion of Satan.
5.  **A New Beginning (Jesus Christ):** The life, death, and resurrection of Jesus as the ultimate revelation of God.
6.  **Bearing Kingdom Fruit:** Cooperating with the Holy Spirit and living out one's identity in Christ.

---

## 2. Bilingual Learning Requirements
YWAM Sendai operates as a bilingual environment. The weekly learning schedule is adjusted to ensure high-quality engagement for both English and Japanese speakers:
* **Standard Weekly Hours:** 50 total learning hours.
* **Language Engagement:** A minimum of 16 hours of direct content engagement is required for a bi-lingual school (compared to 12 hours in single-language schools).

---

## 3. Skills to be Developed
Throughout the curriculum, students engage in:
* **Spiritual Disciplines:** Intercession, worship, and engaging with Scripture.
* **Relational Skills:** Teamwork, hospitality, and cross-cultural communication.
* **Practical Stewardship:** Responsible management of time, health, and finances.
