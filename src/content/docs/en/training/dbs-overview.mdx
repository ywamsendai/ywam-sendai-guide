The Discipleship Bible School (DBS) is a 12-week immersive journey through the 66 books of the Bible. Rather than just studying *about* the Word, you will read through the entire Bible chronologically, discovering God’s Redemptive Plan from Genesis to Revelation.

## What is the DBS?
The DBS is designed for those who want to build their lives on the solid foundation of God’s Word. Over three months, you will:
* **Read Cover-to-Cover:** Follow the historical narrative of Scripture in the order events occurred.
* **Understand Context:** Study the genre, historical setting, and original intent of each author.
* **Observe Harmony:** Discover how the Old and New Testaments form one unified story of redemption.

## The Learning Environment
* **Chronological Approach:** We study the books in the order they were written/occurred to understand the flow of history.
* **Small Group Processing:** Engage in discussions to apply Biblical truths to modern life.
* **Inductive Method:** Learn to ask critical questions of the text to identify original meaning versus personal bias.

<Aside type="tip" title="Outreach Opportunity">
After completing the 12-week lecture phase, students may have the opportunity to join a cross-cultural outreach to share the Word with the nations.
</Aside>

## Is the DBS for me?
If you have completed a YWAM DTS and want to deepen your Biblical literacy and leadership skills, the DBS provides the tools to handle the Word of God with confidence and integrity.
